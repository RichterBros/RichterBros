About Me

Working with a variety of clients in varying industries, I am well versed with producing explanatory content that transforms each client’s message into an engaging visual form of communication best suited for their industry. As the owner of my own company and working as a freelance artist within it, I balance time management while pushing the creative to the highest level. I am able to manage myself quite independently, establishing and meeting deadlines with clients while providing a sufficient amount of iterations in collaboration with my clients’ needs.

My Background

Born and raised in Portland, Oregon. I attended Pacific Northwest college of Art from 1995 - 1988, focusing on graphic design, Fine art painting and sculpture. From there I started a business with my brother creating animations for LED signs all accross the US and abroad. From 2014 -2016 I worked as a senior motion graphics designer for Downstream. 2017-present: freelancing and attending code school to expand my skillset.
